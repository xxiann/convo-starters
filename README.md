# Go:NE conversation starters

A simple web-based conversation card game designed for **community hangouts, small groups**.

Players take turns passing the phone around and answering prompts that gradually move from **light conversation → deeper reflection**.

Feel free to give more suggestions on this [Google Form](https://forms.gle/BxsAaiVx2rjzbXkb6)

All thanks to ChatGPT and the databases they are trained on :)

## How its played

1. Choose **categories** of questions.
2. Select how many **Easy / Medium / Hard** cards you want.
3. Press **GO** to generate a deck.
4. Pass the phone around and take turns answering the prompts.
